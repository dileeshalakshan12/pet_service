Our story

Built by pet people, for pet people.

Pawstop started with a simple frustration: finding trustworthy pet care shouldn't mean a dozen phone calls and no way to compare who's actually good. So we built a marketplace where every provider is reviewed, every booking is tracked, and every pet gets a proper profile.

🎯 Our Mission

Make quality pet care as easy to book as a restaurant table — transparent pricing, real reviews, instant confirmation.

🤝 For Providers

We give grooming studios, vets, walkers, and sitters the tools to manage bookings, post promotions, and grow a loyal following.

🐾 For Pet Parents

Every pet gets a full profile — allergies, medical notes, vaccination status — so providers always have what they need.
